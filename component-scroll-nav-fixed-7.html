Health and wellbeing

Your well-being matters. From our on-site health and wellness center, physical therapy and sports medicine care, and state-of-the-art fitness center, we're here to support your wellness goals. We also offer well-being spaces for meditation, prayer and reflection, because we know health includes your mind and spirit, too.

Health and Wellness Center

Located on the 28th floor, the Health & Wellness Center provides quick, convenient and confidential medical care to U.S. benefits-eligible employees. In addition to sick visits and acute care, licensed providers also offer general medical advice, lab work, allergy shot management and international travel preparation. Walk in or call ahead at 212-270-5555.

Learn more about the range of services offered.

Until the building is fully open, only employees located at 270 Park will be able to use the Health and Wellness Center on the 28th floor.

All other employees should use the 277 Park Health & Wellness Center.
