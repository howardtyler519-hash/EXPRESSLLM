Overview

Last updated: February 2026  ·  IT Support  ·  KB0010042

This article explains the complete process for completing this task from start to finish. Read through each section before beginning to make sure you have everything you need.

This process applies to all full-time employees. Contractors should contact their project manager for the appropriate workflow.

💡 Note: This process was updated in February 2026. If you completed this process before that date, some steps may look different.

Requirements

Make sure you have the following before you begin.

Access & Permissions

  • Active company account with standard employee access
  • VPN connection if working remotely
  • Manager approval if your role requires it — check with your supervisor

Required Materials

  • Your employee ID number
  • Any relevant reference numbers or case IDs
  • Supporting documentation (if applicable)

Step-by-Step

Follow these steps in order. Do not skip ahead.

1
Log into the portalNavigate to the company intranet and sign in with your standard credentials. If you cannot log in, contact the IT helpdesk before continuing.
2
Navigate to the correct sectionFrom the homepage, select My Requests from the top navigation, then choose New Request from the dropdown.
3
Complete all required fieldsFill in every field marked with an asterisk (*). Leave optional fields blank if you are unsure — you can update them later.
4
Submit and record your confirmation numberClick Submit and note the confirmation number shown on the next screen. You will need this if you contact support.
⚠ Important: Submissions are final once sent. Double-check all fields before clicking Submit.

Troubleshooting

Common issues and how to resolve them.

I cannot log into the portal

Make sure you are connected to the VPN if working remotely. If your password has expired, use the self-service reset tool at the login screen. If neither works, contact IT at extension 4401.

My submission is not going through

Check that all required fields are filled in and that your attachments are under the 10MB file size limit. Clear your browser cache and try again. If the issue persists, use a different browser.

I did not receive a confirmation email

Check your spam or junk folder. Allow up to 15 minutes for the email to arrive. If you still have not received it, log back in and check My Requests to confirm the submission went through.
